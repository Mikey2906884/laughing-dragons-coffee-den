About Us

Humble Beginnings

Welcome to Laughing Dragons Coffee Den, where the enchanting world of fantasy and the rich aroma of freshly brewed coffee collide! Nestled in the heart of our vibrant community, Laughing Dragons was founded by Michael and Alyssa, a passionate couple of Dungeons and Dragons enthusiasts. The story of Laughing Dragons began during late-night gaming sessions filled with epic quests, heroic battles, and laughter around the table, but the magic truly began with the transformation of an old book store into our fantastical coffee shop.

Our Dream

At Laughing Dragons, we believe that every cup has a tale to tell. Our menu is filled with creatively named drinks and treats that nod to iconic themes of D&D. But we are more than just a coffee shop! Laughing Dragons is a community hub, where we dream to forge friendships over shared interests. Join us for weekly game nights, themed events and special workshops to elevate your D&D experience!
